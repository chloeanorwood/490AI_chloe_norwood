AI in Economics • Fall 2024

Project Overview

This project explores the application of artificial intelligence techniques within managerial economics and business decision-making.

The focus is on developing practical AI tools that help organizations analyze data, forecast outcomes, and design better strategies in real-world markets.

Using a blend of theory and implementation, the project demonstrates how machine learning, predictive analytics, and automation can support more informed and efficient economic decisions.
